About the project

FocusInfinity is your go-to productivity Chrome Extension which will help you be at the top of your game both at work or your classes. You might have all the resources you need to get the best grades or to finish your projet on time - but sometimes all you might be lacking, must be the productivity. That's where FocusInfinity comes in! Now you don't have to worry about your time wasted: instead just set a timer, set the perfect mood with our vast collection of Ambient Sounds, make a To-Do list of all your tasks and get going!

Features.

What you can do on FocusInfinity.

Set a Timer

No need to clutter your desk with another clock, or another device to set a timer. Time your work, make every second count.

Ambient Sounds

How amazing would it feel to study with the sounds of the rain pouring outside your window...in the month of January! Studies also show that playing some kind of music in the background may help you focus better.

To-Do List

Never miss a task that you had at the back of your mind ever again. Set up your to-do list, update it as you want.

Simple UI

We know that tech isn't for everyone. And using FocusInfinity for your productivity is a cakewalk! Simple UI and UX.
